The Voles

The Voles, Their music, a distinctive genre they've coined 'spaghetti grunge,' is as much a nod to their playful experimentation as it is to their intertwined histories. Despite their current hiatus, The Voles' foray into the music scene has left a series of tracks that reverberate with the raw energy and unpolished edge reminiscent of a genre born in garages and bred in basements. The Voles' musical journey may have paused, but the tracks laid down are alive with the thrum of potential, waiting for the moment when these five friends pick up where they left off.
